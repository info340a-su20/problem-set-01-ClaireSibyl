Desir Arman

Desir Arman is the protagonist from the manhwa "A Beginner's Magic Should Be Special" authored by Yu So Nan. The artist is Uk Jag Ga and the publishers are D&C Media and KakaoPage. Set in a fantasy world right after defeating the biggest threat to mankind that has wiped out millions of people including his friends and lover, Desir and his party get wiped out after forgetting the body of their enemy would have a high impact explosion. However, instead of dying, Desir discovers that he was sent decades back into the past, where he now vows to redo his life but this time with as little death as possible.

  • Smart, mature, and witty character
  • Hardworking, earnest, and sincere
  • Badass and talented
  • Builds others up
